05 — Sub-Brands

The Product Family

Each product shares the dark foundation and forge orange, but has its own accent colour, background tint, and visual personality.

Orbit

Track every mission to the stars.

Space mission tracker. Deep navy backgrounds, sky blue accent, forge orange ring — a visual metaphor for the orange-lit rocket exhaust against the blue of space.

QuizForge

The pub quiz platform, forged.

Pub quiz platform. Deep purple backgrounds, violet accent, forge orange lightning bolt — quiz knowledge (speech bubble) meets the forge (lightning bolt).

06 — Accessibility

Contrast Ratios

WCAG 2.1 contrast requirements: AA requires 4.5:1 for normal text, 3:1 for large text (18pt+ or 14pt bold). AAA requires 7:1.
